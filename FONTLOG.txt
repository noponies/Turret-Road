Basic Font Information

Tracer is an agular geometric sans serif typeface, with very low contrast, high x-height and gylph height, and playful diacritics.

Tracer delves into eugenics, solar evolution, science fiction and homeward conversations.

More detailed information can be found here: https://noponies.github.io/tracer/
